About Me

I’m Justice Muhammed Okan, writing as Stephen Muhammed, hailing from Auchi, Edo State, Nigeria, where I started scribbling tales at 16, driven by the glaring gap in Afrofuturistic stories on the shelves.

With a degree in Educational Administration and Planning from the University of Abuja, I now teach in a secondary school, dreaming up worlds between lesson plans.

I am fueled by a mission to flood the market with African futures—and it’s just the first spark in a blazing universe of interconnected books.
